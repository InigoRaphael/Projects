Swimmer

My most edge in terms of sports. I loved the water when I was still 3 y/o. I got my talent because of my father. I am somewhat competitive in this sport. My soul feel free when in the water. Swimming has been part of my life, it molded me and changed my character. That is why I have no reason to quit.
